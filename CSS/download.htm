Haval Pakistan Slashes Prices by Rs4 Lac for All Haval H6 Trims

October 27, 2023October 27, 2023

Haval Pakistan has implemented a significant price reduction for all trim levels of the Haval H6. This move comes in response to the recent strengthening of the Pakistani Rupee against the US Dollar.

The Haval H6, a compact SUV, is proudly manufactured locally by Sazgar Engineering Works Limited in Pakistan. Its success in the Pakistani market can be attributed to its innovative lightweight technology, which enhances fuel efficiency.

This SUV is renowned for its contemporary aesthetics, spacious interior, and an array of premium SUV features that contribute to its stylish and modern design. Inside, you’ll discover a generously proportioned and well-appointed cabin that seamlessly combines comfort with sophistication.

Price adjustments are as follows:

The ex-factory price of the Haval H6 1.5T has been reduced to Rs9,099,000 from its previous price of Rs9,499,000, marking a price reduction of Rs400,000.

The ex-factory price of the Haval H6 2.0T AWD is now Rs10,449,000, down from Rs10,849,000, reflecting a price reduction of Rs400,000.

The ex-factory price of the Haval H6 HEV is now Rs7,949,000, a significant reduction from its previous price of Rs12,149,000, indicating a price reduction of Rs400,000.

Available Exterior Colors:

The Haval H6 HEV is offered in a range of seven distinct colors to cater to various preferences and styles. These color options include Blue, Black, Green, Grey, White, Red, and Dark Red.

Engine:

The Haval H6 is equipped with a hybrid engine, combining fuel efficiency and performance. Its hybrid configuration strikes a balance between power and eco-friendliness and incorporates a single-turbocharger to enhance overall performance.

With a 1497 cc displacement, the Haval H6’s engine features four cylinders arranged in an inline configuration, optimizing combustion efficiency and promoting smooth operation. The H6 is a front-wheel drive (FWD) vehicle, providing enhanced handling and maneuverability.

The engine of the Haval H6 features a double overhead camshaft (DOHC) valve mechanism and utilizes four valves per cylinder to improve airflow and combustion efficiency. This engine generates a maximum horsepower of 240 HP at 5500 RPM, ensuring ample acceleration and performance. Additionally, it produces a torque of 530 Nm at 4400 RPM, offering strong pulling power and responsiveness. The SUV has a fuel tank capacity of 55 liters.

Exterior:

At the front, the SUV boasts a prominent grille with chrome accents, creating a bold and aggressive appearance. The sleek and angular headlights incorporate LED lighting technology to enhance visibility.

The bumper design combines body-colored elements, black accents, and integrated fog lights. The adjustable headlights allow customization of illumination according to driver preference and road conditions.

Daytime running lights (DRLs) improve visibility for other drivers, ensuring the car remains easily noticeable on the road. Moving to the sides, the Haval H6 exhibits a streamlined and muscular profile with sculpted body lines, imparting a dynamic and aerodynamic appearance.

The vehicle rides on stylish 19-inch alloy wheels with a tire size of 225/55, striking a balance between comfort and grip on various road surfaces. The colored outside door handles seamlessly blend with the vehicle’s body, creating a sleek and cohesive look.

Side mirrors with indicators enhance safety by signaling the car’s intentions to nearby vehicles. Front and rear boneless automatic wipers activate upon detecting rain to ensure optimum visibility during rainy conditions.

A sunroof and moonroof flood the cabin with natural light, while roof rails add practicality and versatility for transporting additional luggage or equipment.

At the rear, the SUV showcases a well-defined and sporty design. The taillights feature LED technology and a distinctive shape that complements the overall vehicle aesthetics. The rear bumper integrates elements like reflectors and exhaust outlets.

The car is equipped with an electric tailgate featuring a convenient delayed closing and memory function, simplifying loading and unloading activities. A rear spoiler adds a sporty and aerodynamic touch.

Interior:

The Haval H6 offers a choice of interior colors, including orange, black, grey, and red. The interior provides a comfortable and modern experience, designed to meet the needs of its occupants. With a sleek and ergonomic dashboard, the driver-centric layout offers easy access to controls and a clear instrument cluster for essential information.

A touchscreen infotainment system positioned prominently on the center console serves as the hub for various functions, including navigation, audio controls, and connectivity features. The seating and upholstery prioritize comfort and support, with options for power-adjustable seats, heating, and ventilation in higher trim levels.

The interior also offers ample storage space, with compartments for personal items, cup holders, and a spacious cargo area.

Safety Features:

The Haval H6 is equipped with a comprehensive range of safety features to ensure the well-being of its occupants. These include six airbags for comprehensive collision protection. The speed-sensing auto door lock enhances security by automatically locking the doors at a certain speed.

The H6 features five seatbelts, accompanied by driver and passenger seat belt warning systems to encourage proper usage. Additional safety features include Down Hill Assist Control and Hill Start Assist Control for maintaining control on hilly terrains. The immobilizer ensures the vehicle cannot be started without the correct key.

Traction control improves stability and grip on slippery surfaces. The H6 also includes door opening warnings, vehicle stability control, child locks, rear fog lamps, and ISOFIX child seat anchors for added safety and convenience.

Advanced safety technologies such as Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB), Blind Spot Detection (BSD), Lane Departure Warning System (LDWS), Lane Keep Assist System (LKAS), and Electronic Brake-Force Distribution (EBD) are present. The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS), Brake Assist (BA), and High Mount Stop Lamp further contribute to the overall safety of the Haval H6.
